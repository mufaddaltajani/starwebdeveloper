Note on Cloudflare Keys
The 1x00... keys I provided in the code above are Cloudflare's official Testing Keys. They will automatically generate a successful verification for you locally without needing to set up an account immediately.
When you deploy to production:
- Go to your Cloudflare Dashboard -> Turnstile.
- Add your domain.
- Replace the data-sitekey in login.php with the generated Site Key.
- Replace the $turnstileSecret in AuthController.php with the generated Secret Key.
